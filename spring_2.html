Spring

season of rebirth.

Natures clock

The year begins in January, middle of the winter season, which carries on into February. Milder temperatures in March mark the begining of spring, and continue through April into May.

Summer is anounced in the month of June, with a notable increase in temperature, leading to pleasent outdoor conditions through July into August.

In September however, temperatures begin to fall, anouncing the start of Autumn. As the warmth slowley disappears through the months of October and November, we are reminded that winter is approaching and its time to put on our warm coats, in time for it's arrivel in the month of December!

Season

In Focus

Before Spring was called Spring, it was called Lent in Old English. Starting in the 14th century, that time of year was called “springing time”—a reference to plants “springing” from the ground. In the 15th century this got shortened to “spring-time,” and then further shortened in the 16th century to just “spring.”
